# Facebook App Guide

## Navigation
- **Bottom Navigation Bar** (5 tabs):
  - Home (Feed): News feed
  - Watch: Video section - TAP THIS for video content
  - Marketplace: Shopping
  - Notifications: Bell icon
  - Menu: Three lines icon

## Watch Section
- **Access**: Tap "Watch" tab in bottom navigation (usually 2nd icon from left)
- **Video Feed**: Vertical scrolling feed of videos
- **Scroll**: Swipe UP to go to next video (swipe from bottom to top)
- **Like Button**:
  - Usually on the RIGHT side of video
  - Icon: Thumbs up or heart icon
  - Turns blue/filled when liked
  - May also be below the video

## Common Actions
- **Like a video**:
  1. Find the like/thumbs up icon (usually right side or below video)
  2. Tap once to like
  3. Icon changes color to indicate liked state

- **Scroll to next video**:
  - Swipe UP (start from middle/bottom, swipe towards top)
  - Video will automatically play when in view

- **Navigate to Watch**:
  1. Look for bottom navigation bar
  2. Tap the "Watch" icon (video play icon, usually 2nd position)

## UI Patterns
- Videos auto-play when in view
- Like buttons are interactive and show visual feedback
- Bottom nav bar is always visible
- Swipe gestures are smooth and responsive

## Package Name
`com.facebook.katana`

## Tips for Automation
- Wait for video to load before liking
- Like button position may vary but usually on right side or below video
- Use swipe gesture (not tap) to move between videos
- Bottom navigation is consistent across app
